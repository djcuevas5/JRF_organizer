📋 How to Process an Order

  1. Review the Sales Order – Check that all required fields (Job ID, Cat #, Type) are filled.
  2. Verify the Checklist – Ensure eVault, RFA Missing, Key Interlocks, QA Checklist, Artwork Approved, and Drawings in sp_hold are correctly marked.
  3. Check Dates – Confirm the Checked Date and Committed Date are set.
  4. Fill Missing / In Process – If something is missing or in progress, add details in the respective text fields.
  5. Complete the Order – Once everything is ready and the card turns green, click the Complete button to remove it from the hold queue.

Tip: Use the filters (VISTA / PME) to focus on specific product lines.
